Rules of Substitutions

  • The swap of players has to be amongst the same category.
  • Substituted players will NOT get bonus points. Bonus points include +1x of captain, +0.5x of VC, century, half-century, 4 wicket haul, and 5 wicket haul points. If a user substitutes his captain or vice-captain, he will get normal points and not 2x or 1.5x points
  • Each player can make only one substitution per match and will only come into play after the innings break.
  • This substitution is optional. Even one teammate from a team can do it independently if the other teammate doesn't want to.
